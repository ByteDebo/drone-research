# Drone Defence Research Portal

Research repository for autonomous drone interceptor systems — low-cost, AI-guided, China-free counter-drone defence technology.

## What's Inside

All pages are **AES-256 encrypted** and require a password to access.

## Research Areas Covered

- Ukrainian combat-proven interceptor drones (STING, P1-SUN, STRILA, JEDI, Tenebris)
- Autonomous terminal guidance in GPS-denied / jammed environments
- Acoustic detection networks ($15/node, 2-5km range)
- Thermal + visual AI guidance modules (ESP32/STM32 + MLX90640/FLIR Lepton)
- Edge AI for real-time target tracking (YOLO, VIO, sensor fusion)
- India defence manufacturing ecosystem and indigenisation roadmap
- NDAA Section 889 compliance and Blue UAS pathway
- Defence market sizing and competitor analysis (Anduril, Shield AI, Helsing, DroneShield, Fortem)
